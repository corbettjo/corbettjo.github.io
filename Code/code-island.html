Forbidden Island

Forbidden Island is a multiplayer flood survival game that was a two week assignment for CS2500 (Fundamentals of Computer Science II).

It was written in Java, and was the first time in this course we were given some flexibility in designing an application. We were given the general rules and algorithms for terrain generation, but the rest was open ended and up to my partner and I.

We had to make 3 different terrrain styles, seen above: A perfect diamond, random terrain diamond, and random terrain islands. The goal of the game is to collect all tokens and get to the helicopter before the island floods.

The color of the tile corresponds to its height on the map. The lighter the tile, the higher it is. Head over here for more info.
